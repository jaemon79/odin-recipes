Deviled Eggs Recipe

Avocado Deviled Eggs

Recipe by thehealthyhousewife (allrecipes.com)

A "Californian" twist on a traditional recipe

Sacrelige!? No, scrupmtiousness. Green is the new yellow. Kick it up a notch at the potluck by swapping out some of the bad fat for the good fat.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and halved
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 3 slices cooked turkey bacon, chopped, divided
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • sea salt to taste
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, sliced (optional)
  • 1 dash hot sauce, or to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. Scoop egg yokes into a bowl; add avocado, 2/3 of chopped turkey bacon, mayonnaise, lime juice, garlic, cayenne pepper, and salt
  2. Mash egg yolk mixture until filling is evenly combined
  3. Spoon filling into a piping bag or plastic bag with a snipped corner
  4. Pipe filling into each egg white; top with a turkey bacon piece, jalapeno slice, and dash hot sauce
